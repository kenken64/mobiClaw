Connect Your Android Device

Follow these steps to get started

1

Enable Developer Options

Settings > About Phone > tap Build Number 7 times

2

Enable USB Debugging

Settings > Developer Options > turn on USB Debugging

3

Connect via USB

Plug in your phone with a data cable (not charge-only). Tap Allow on the USB debugging prompt.

4

Start Mirroring

Select your device from the dropdown on the left and click Start Mirror

Or use Wireless ADB on the left panel to connect without USB
